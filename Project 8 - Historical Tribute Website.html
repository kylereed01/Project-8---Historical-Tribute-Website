General Douglas MacArthur



About "Dugout Doug" MacArthur

January 26, 1880 - April 5, 1964

- MacArthur was born on January 26th, 1880 in Little Rock, Arkansas. He graduated as the top student from West Point Military Academy in 1903.

- MacArthur served as a 5-star general in WWII. His squadron was forced to evacuate the Philippines in 1942, but he later led efforts that directly resulted in Japan's surrender.

- After WWII, MacArthur was responsible for overseeing the United States occupation of Japan and was a key figure in creating democratic reforms in the nation.

- MacArthur was appointed as Commander of United Nations forces during the Korean War. He led a controversial military assault on key territories in Korea, however, this action resulted in his dismissal by President Truman.
